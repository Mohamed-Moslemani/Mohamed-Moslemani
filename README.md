## About Me

I build production-grade AI systems with a strong foundation in machine learning research, computational science, and systems engineering.

My work focuses on bridging theoretical machine learning with deployable infrastructure, scalable architectures, and enterprise-grade automation systems.

Currently working as an **AI Engineer at Strategy& (PwC Network)**, focusing on:

* Forecasting and predictive systems
* Production ML pipelines
* Agentic and multi-agent systems
* Enterprise AI automation
* LLM-powered retrieval and orchestration systems
* Data virtualization and distributed workflows
* Observability, tracing, and deployment infrastructure

Research remains central to how I approach engineering.

My interests revolve around:

* Distribution shifts and model robustness
* Generalization under changing environments
* Numerical optimization
* Scientific computing
* Intelligent system behavior
* Chaos theory and emergent dynamics in AI systems

Currently pursuing a **Master's in Computational Science** at the **American University of Beirut (AUB)** on a full scholarship, while serving as a Graduate Assistant and Assistant Instructor for Data Science courses in Python and R.
