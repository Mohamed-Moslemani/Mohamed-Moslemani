## About Me

I build production-grade AI systems with a strong foundation in machine learning research, computational science, and systems engineering.

My work focuses on designing intelligent systems that operate reliably beyond research environments, bridging theoretical modeling with scalable infrastructure, deployment architecture, and enterprise-grade automation.

Currently working as an data scientist at **Strategy&**, part of the PwC Network, focusing on:

* Forecasting and predictive systems
* Production ML pipelines
* Agentic and multi-agent systems
* Enterprise AI automation
* LLM-powered retrieval and orchestration systems
* Data virtualization and distributed workflows
* Observability, tracing, and deployment infrastructure

Research remains central to how I think and build.

My interests revolve around:

* Distribution shifts and model robustness
* Generalization under changing environments
* Numerical optimization
* Scientific computing
* Intelligent system behavior
* Chaos theory and emergent dynamics in AI systems

I am currently pursuing a Master's in Computational Science at the American University of Beirut (AUB) on a full scholarship, while serving as a Graduate Assistant and Assistant Instructor for Data Science courses in Python and R.
